Help & Support - AttendEase

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I register my face in AttendEase?
A: Go to your dashboard → "Add Face" → Capture or Upload your face → Wait for admin approval.

Q: What if my face is not detected?
A: Ensure good lighting and a clear view of your face. Avoid background clutter.

Q: Can faculty scan multiple faces at once?
A: Yes! Faculty can upload up to 3 images for batch recognition in classrooms.

Q: How do I check my attendance?
A: Navigate to "Attendance Records" in your dashboard to see check-in and check-out times.

Troubleshooting

Face Not Recognized: Make sure your face is already registered and approved by the admin.

Login Issues: Reset your password using the "Forgot Password" option.

Attendance Not Marked: Ensure check-in/check-out hasn’t already been recorded today.

System Error (500): Contact support with error details.
